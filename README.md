**No Sweat - COMMUNITY guidelines.**

**At No Sweat ™, our goal is to create a safe, inclusive, and fun environment where you can enjoy gaming and connect with friends. We all play a part in making this community enjoyable for everyone—so let’s work together to keep it positive and welcoming!**

**Always follow** [Epic Terms & Service](https://legal.epicgames.com/en-US/epicgames/tos)

________________________________________

## **Clan Expectations** / **Section 1**

### Respect and Sportsmanship
- Treat all members with respect, kindness, and fairness.
- Celebrate each other’s successes and support teammates, whether in victory or defeat.
- Avoid trash talk, offensive language, or personal attacks.

### Fair Play / **section 2**
- Play by the [official Fortnite rules](https://safety.epicgames.com/en-US/our-rules) and avoid cheating, hacking, or exploiting bugs.
- Help create a positive gaming experience for everyone.

### Community Focus / **Section 3**
- Our clan **currently** only welcomes members from the **EU region**, as this aligns with the primary community we serve and play with.

### Communication / **Section 4**
- Use respectful language in voice and text chat.
- Listen to clan leaders and follow instructions during clan events or team matches.

### Content Sharing / **Section 5**
- Share only appropriate content within the clan channels.
- Avoid sharing harmful, inappropriate, or offensive material.

---

## **Behaviour Guidelines** / **Section 6** 

- **Be Supportive:** Encourage teammates and promote teamwork.
- **Be Responsible:** Represent the clan positively both in and out of game.
- **Report Issues:** If you see misconduct or rule-breaking, report it to clan leaders.

---
## **Consequences for Violations** / **Section 7**

- **Sanction 1:** Warning  
- **Sanction 2:** 24-hour suspension  
- **Sanction 3:** Meeting with the **Community Manager**  
- **Sanction 4:** 24-hour suspension (repeat or additional violation)  
- **Sanction 5:** Social ban (6-7 days)  
- **Sanction 6:** Community ban (30+ days or permanent)

**If you suspect you have committed a violation or are unsure about your actions, please do not hesitate to contact Support for clarification and guidance.**
